> **Calyx is the heart of the formula for intelligence, implemented as a database.**

## В§9 вЂ” Anti-patterns (refuse these)

- Flattening the panel into one opaque vector (breaks A3 and the whole guard).
- Reporting `C(N,2)` abundance as new information beyond the DPI ceiling (A8).
- Labeling bits/kernel/guard "trusted" without grounding (A2) вЂ” say `provisional`.
- Mutating a frozen lens, or training on synthetic generator output (model collapse).
- Substituting an external theory of intelligence for the Royse corpus (В§2).
- Making lens plug-in, bits, or kernel harder to reach (В§5).
- A green-checkmark harness standing in for FSV (В§0).
- **Validating, benching, or FSV-ing a retrieval/recall/signal/fusion/scale/SLO gate on fewer than 10 embedders or on synthetic vectors (A35).** A single lens is a vector DB, not the Constellation; 1вЂ“2 lenses cannot even be *valued* (value is associational вЂ” PID over в‰Ґ 3, stable over в‰Ґ 10). Every such run uses a real panel of **в‰Ґ 10 lenses** (scale toward 20+, resource-aware) and records per-lens bits + the ensemble decomposition + the fused result, or it fails closed. (The retired 4-lens bootstrap floor no longer satisfies any gate.)
- **Counting to 10 with redundant near-clones вЂ” e.g. ten general-purpose dense semantic embedders вЂ” and calling the panel diverse (A37).** The в‰Ґ 10 count gate (A35) and the pairwise в‰¤ 0.6 contract (A7) are both *necessary but not sufficient*: 10 single-family models maximize redundancy (their PID bits are redundant, not unique/synergistic) and a panel can be collectively rank-deficient while every pair sits under 0.6. A gate-bearing panel MUST also span distinct association *families* and clear a **panel-level** redundancy bound (`n_eff`, mean corr/NMI, non-collapsing marginal-bits). A 10-by-count-but-redundant panel is diagnostic/control-only and fails closed for gates (A16).
- A code/test file over 500 lines without a tracking GitHub issue to modularize it (В§8); or modularizing in a way that breaks the public API or adds a circular dependency.
- Bolting on a separate search/graph/vector DB instead of using the Association Engine that subsumes them (В§3).
